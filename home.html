SEASON THE MOMENT

At Jasiba, we believe in everything at its prime. Our focus is to bring the best and most responsibly sourced ingredients to the table.


Jasiba is a contemporary African style showcasing techniques rooted in Kenya but influenced by our regionality, carefully sourcing only the best ingredients from both the tropical & abroad. Building off the vibrant food scene of Nairobian Neo-bistro movement, Jasiba displays an inspiring take on African cuisine in a relaxing environment. This enables our genuine approach to food & hospitality to shine.



Jasiba Hotel was recognized with one star in the 2019, 2020 & 2021 Michelin guides.
